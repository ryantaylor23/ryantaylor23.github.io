Have you ever been Hooked on Fishing?

When I was younger, I had my first fishing experience with my father. As I stepped into the rushing river, I walked carefully to my spot where I thought the fish would be. I cast my line out. I watched my fly as it floated on top of the water running downstream. As I was about to pull my fly up to recast again, Snatch! A fish snatched my fly right off of the top of the water. The adrenaline rushed through my body as I reeled in the fish. After we let the fish go and I caught my breath I realized in that moment, I was hooked on fishing.
